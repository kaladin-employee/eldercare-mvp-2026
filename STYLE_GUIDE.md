# STYLE_GUIDE.md\n\n## Summary of Apple HIG/Ive Principles\n\n### Simplicity\n- Remove clutter to keep the interface clean and uncluttered.\n- Use minimal elements to convey necessary information without unnecessary distractions.\n\n### Clarity\n- Ensure text is legible with good contrast ratios and appropriate font sizes.\n- Make sure icons are clear and easy to understand at a glance.\n\n### Deference\n- Prioritize the user’s task by making primary actions prominent and secondary actions less obtrusive.\n- Use subtle animations to enhance usability without overwhelming the user.\n\n### Depth\n- Apply subtle motion effects, such as hover states or focus outlines, to provide feedback and indicate interactivity.\n- Use shadows and gradients for depth but ensure they do not create visual clutter.\n\n### Harmony\n- Design elements to be adaptive and responsive, ensuring consistent behavior across different device sizes and orientations.\n- Ensure color schemes are harmonious and follow Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines.
