# ElderCareApp Doctrine (locked from sunset memo)
- Reassurance tool, not medical/911.
- PWA-first on Cloudflare.
- Elder Station (dedicated Android phone) + Caregiver PWA.
- One-tap Alert, Daily Check-In, ACK, escalation ladder.
- Reliability transparency: Last Seen, System Health, Test Alerts.
- QR pairing, 2-week trial.
- Scope: no SMS, no BLE, no GPS tracking.
